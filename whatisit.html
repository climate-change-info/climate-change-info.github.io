Climate Change


What is it?


"Climate change is a change in the pattern of weather, and related changes in oceans, land surfaces and ice sheets, occurring over time scales of decades or longer"

- Australian Academy of Science

Short Definition

Climate change is a long term change of climate patterns. Changes in the climate of Earth have been examined since the 1900s. One of the main factors that affect climate change is human activity. This includes but is not limited to: fossil fuel burning, farming livestock and deforestation.
